This Is All About Rajasthan!

Established/Formation: The state of Rajasthan was formed on 30th March 1949. Rajasthan Day or Rajasthan Foundation Day is celebrated every year on 30th March with great pomp.

Area and District: The area of Rajasthan state is 3,42,239 square kilometer and the western part of this state is also the largest desert part of India. At present there are 33 districts in Rajasthan state.


Language: The official language language of Rajasthanis Hindi. The regional languages are Rajasthani and Mewati. English language is an additional language of this state.

Costume/Dress: Rajasthani dress of Rajasthan is a very unique and beautiful dress. Different castes live here and clothes are also worn according to the caste. To protect from the heat of the sun and to maintain dignity, women use a piece of cloth to cover the head, which is also called Odhni or Chunni. Women's dress is usually designed in bright colors like blue, yellow and orange. Elders and elders prefer to wear white and colored turban, dhoti and kurta.


Music: Folk music is a big part of Rajasthani culture. Religious or devotional songs, also known as bhajans. These songs and bhajans are often sung with musical instruments like dholak, sitar and sarangi. The Manganiyar and Langa communities of Rajasthan are famous for their folk music.


Dance:Ghoomar dance of Rajasthan state has got international recognition. Puppet dance, Bhopa dance, Chang dance, Teratali dance, Gair dance and Kutchi Ghodi dance are the famous dances of Rajasthan.


Festivals: Gangaur, Gaj, Teej, Dussehra, Holi, Diwali, Makar Sankranti, Raksha Bandhan, Janmashtami festival etc. are the major festivals of Rajasthan.


Food/Dishes: Bajra roti, garlic chutney, mawa kachori, onion kachori and mirchi kachori are very famous dishes of Rajasthan state. Apart from this, Ghevar of Jodhpur, Mawa of Alwar, Kadhi Kachori of Ajmer, Malpua of Pushkar, Dal Kachori of Kota and Bikaneri Bhujia and Rasgulla of Bikaner are famous for delicious dishes all over India.

Tourist places:Rajasthan state is one of the important tourist places of India. Various places of Rajasthan have been the center of special attraction for tourists for many years. There are many wonderful forts and palaces here. Every year thousands of tourists come to visit Rajasthan state. Name of some important/famous tourist places of Rajasthan state:- Jal Mahal, Hawa Mahal, Chandra Mahal, Jantar Mantar, Amer Fort, Jaigarh Fort, City Palace, Rambagh Palace etc.
